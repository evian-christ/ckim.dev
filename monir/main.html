Monir Support

Find answers to frequently asked questions and contact support.

🧾 What is Monir?

Monir is a lightweight and intuitive app to track daily expenses and manage your budget with minimal effort.

💾 Where is my data stored?

Your data is stored securely on your device and optionally backed up via iCloud if enabled in settings.

💳 What is Monir Pro?

Monir Pro offers premium features like recurring expenses, advanced insights, and data export/import.

🔁 Can I restore purchases?

Yes! You can restore previous purchases from the General Settings page under “Restore Purchases”.

📱 Is Monir available on iPad?

Monir is currently optimized for iPhone. iPad support is not officially provided.
